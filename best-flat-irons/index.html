Best for fine or heat‑sensitive hair

If your hair is thin, delicate, or prone to damage, the ghd Platinum+ Styler is the safest choice because it maintains a steady styling temperature that never spikes. You don’t need to fiddle with a dial, the iron adjusts itself to your hair’s moisture content and pulling speed. That consistency minimises the risk of burning or over‑styling, making it ideal for daily use on fragile strands. Our pick: ghd Platinum+ Professional Hair Straightener.

Best for thick or coarse hair

For thick, curly, or coarse hair that demands more heat and pressure to straighten, the BaByliss PRO Nano Titanium Flat Iron delivers. Its titanium plates heat up fast and hold their temperature even during back‑to‑back passes. The wide‑plate version covers more area per stroke, cutting down styling time significantly. Best for travel and on‑the‑go styling

When you need a straightener that works anywhere without hunting for an outlet, the Dyson Corrale is unmatched. Its cordless design lets you style in a hotel room, car, or event space with full freedom of movement. The flexible plates also mean fewer passes, which is a bonus when you’re short on time. Our verdict

For most people, the ghd Platinum+ Styler remains the flat iron that balances performance, safety, and longevity better than any other, it’s an investment that pays off in healthier‑looking hair day after day. If your budget is tighter or your hair is particularly thick, the CHI G2 and BaByliss PRO respectively are smart alternatives that won’t let you down. And if cordless freedom is non‑negotiable, the Dyson Corrale is a beautifully engineered splurge. Whichever you choose, pair it with a good heat protectant and your strands will thank you.
