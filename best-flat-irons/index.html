A great flat iron can transform your daily routine, but the wrong one can leave hair fried or frizzy. The key is matching the plate material, temperature range, and width to your hair type and styling habits. Ceramic plates distribute heat evenly for fine to medium hair, while titanium heats up fast and stays hot for thick or coarse strands. Adjustable temperature settings let you dial in the right heat, and features like floating plates and automatic shutoff add convenience and safety.
